NEWS APPLICATION

May 2023 - Jun 2023

News Application | Android Project

The news application I created is designed to provide users with up-to-date news content fetched from an API. The application utilizes a RecyclerView to display a list of news articles, and each item in the list shows a brief overview of the news story. When the user clicks on a specific news article in the RecyclerView, a click listener is triggered, which opens a new activity dedicated to displaying the full content of the selected news article. This new activity provides a more comprehensive view of the news story, allowing users to read the complete article and access any related multimedia content.

What Can I Do For You?

Experienced in Mobile App Development, Web Development, UI/UX Designing, and Freelancing. If you have any related work, feel free to reach out to me.

Webdesign

Web design is the process of creating visually appealing and user-friendly websites. It involves a combination of artistic elements, user experience considerations, and technical skills to craft websites that engage and captivate visitors.

Web Development

Web development is the process of creating and maintaining websites or web applications. It involves a combination of front-end and back-end development to ensure a fully functional and user-friendly online presence. Web developers use various programming languages, frameworks, and tools to build websites that cater to different business needs and user requirements.

App Development

App development refers to the process of creating applications (apps) that run on various platforms, such as mobile devices, desktop computers, or web browsers. It involves designing, building, testing, and deploying software applications to meet specific user needs and address particular problems or challenges.

UI/UX Design

UI/UX design refers to the process of creating user interfaces and user experiences for digital products, such as websites, mobile apps, software, and more. UI (User Interface) design focuses on the visual appearance and layout of the product, while UX (User Experience) design concentrates on ensuring the product is easy to use, engaging, and meets users' needs effectively.

Freelancing

Freelancing refers to a work arrangement where individuals, known as freelancers, offer their services to clients or businesses on a project basis. Freelancers are self-employed and are not bound to a long-term contract with a single employer. Instead, they have the flexibility to work for multiple clients simultaneously or take on projects as per their availability and expertise.
